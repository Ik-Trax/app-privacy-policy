Privacy Policy IK Trax Apps

Last Updated: February 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to the IK Trax applications:

  • IK Trax 4003
  • IK Trax EMRx
  • IK Trax EMTx
  • IK Trax EM Tracking

We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Personal Data Collection

These applications do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected.
  • No user accounts are required.
  • No analytics services are used.
  • No advertising SDKs are integrated.
  • No data is transmitted to external servers.

Permissions Required

Location Permission

Accurate location permission is required by Android to enable Bluetooth device discovery and connectivity.

The application does not access, store, or track your physical location. Location permission is solely required by the Android operating system to allow Bluetooth scanning.

Bluetooth Permission

Bluetooth permission is required to connect to compatible IK Trax hardware devices.

The app uses Bluetooth exclusively for:

  • Discovering supported devices
  • Establishing secure device connections
  • Transmitting operational device data

No Bluetooth data is stored externally or transmitted to any remote server.

Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or stored, there is no risk of personal data exposure through these applications.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if application functionality changes. Any updates will be reflected on this page.
